Author Profile

Sreejish Sarada Sreekantan Nair

Asia Book of Records Holder | India Book of Records Holder | Author of Aksharakkadalile Veliyettam

Profile

Indian author and literary documentarian based in the United Arab Emirates.

Sreejish Sarada Sreekantan Nair is recognized for his contributions to Malayalam literature and cultural documentation. His writing is shaped by literary history, global publishing culture, and a commitment to preserving knowledge and experience.

Approach

Research-led, structured, and culturally aware.

Blending analytical depth with literary sensitivity, his work focuses on documenting intellectual heritage, literary movements, and the role of books and literary platforms in society.

Record-Holding Literary Achievement

A milestone in Malayalam literary documentation.

Through Aksharakkadalile Veliyettam, Sreejish recorded and published 128 testimonials in Malayalam, earning official recognition from both the Asia Book of Records and the India Book of Records.

Record Title

Book Details

Aksharakkadalile Veliyettam

The High Tide in the Sea of Letters | Malayalam | 2025 | ISBN 978-93-6167-353-5 | Lipi Publications

Publication

A landmark contribution to Malayalam literary documentation.

The book celebrates literary voices, shared experiences, and the cultural power of books, preserving them within a thoughtfully documented volume.

Significance

A pioneering literary study.

It positions an international book fair as the central subject of an entire book and offers readers a rare perspective on literature, publishing, and cultural exchange.

Book Launch

44th Sharjah International Book Fair

The official release of Aksharakkadalile Veliyettam took place during one of the world’s leading literary and publishing gatherings.

Launch Moment

A memorable celebration of Malayalam literature.

The event connected the book directly with the global atmosphere of literature, publishing, and cultural dialogue that defines Sharjah International Book Fair.
